👥 Who Uses This System

👑
Owner / Manager
Thinks in money — wants to see the big picture
📊 Dashboard 📈 All Reports 📋 Job Order P&L 🏗️ Equipment P&L 🏦 Cash & Bank
✅ Can see everything. ❌ Usually doesn't do data entry — just reviews and approves.
🔢
Accountant / Finance Officer
The backbone — enters and reconciles everything
📒 Chart of Accounts 🧾 Vendor Invoices 💰 Customer Invoices 🏦 Cash & Bank 💳 Payments 📈 Financial Reports ⚖️ Bank Reconciliation 📅 Depreciation
✅ Full access to all financial modules. ❌ Cannot delete posted transactions (audit trail protected).
🦺
Site Manager / Project Manager
Tracks what's happening on the ground
📋 Their Job Orders only 🏗️ Equipment on their site 🔧 Parts used on site 👷 Worker attendance / hours
✅ Can view costs and progress for their own jobs. ❌ Cannot see other job orders, financial ledgers, or full reports.
📦
Procurement Officer
Manages purchasing — vendors, POs, and parts stock
🧾 Vendors 📦 Purchase Orders 🔩 Parts & Spares 📬 Goods Receipt
✅ Can create POs and receive goods. ❌ Cannot post payments or access financial reports — hands off to accountant for payment.
👷
HR / Payroll Admin
Handles employee records and salary processing
👷 Employee Records 💵 Payroll Processing ⏱️ Time & Attendance 📋 Cost Allocation to Jobs
✅ Can run payroll and assign labor costs to job orders / equipment. ❌ Cannot access vendor/customer financials or GL.
