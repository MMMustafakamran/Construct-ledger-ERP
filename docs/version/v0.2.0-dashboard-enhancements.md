# Version 0.2.0 — Dashboard & UI Enhancements

**Date:** 2026-03-28

## Summary

This release adds data visualization, financial intelligence, and usability improvements to the accounting prototype, elevating it from a basic data-entry tool to a closer approximation of a professional accounting dashboard.

## New Features

### Dashboard Enhancements
- **Overdue Invoices Section** — Vendor bills and customer invoices past 30 days are now flagged with days-overdue badges
- **Monthly Cash Flow Bar Chart** — 6-month inflow vs outflow visualization with hover-to-reveal values
- **Invoice Aging Summary** — Three aging buckets (Current 0–30d, Aging 31–60d, Critical 60+d) with progress bars and invoice counts
- **Receivables vs Payables Donut Chart** — SVG donut showing the proportion and net position between AR and AP
- **4th Metric Card** — Overdue invoice count card with danger styling when > 0

### Bank & Cash
- **Transaction History per Account** — Click any bank account row to reveal a chronological list of all payments (outflows) and receipts (inflows) linked to that account
- **Color-coded amounts** — Inflows shown in green, outflows in red, with directional arrows and badges

### Vendors & Customers
- **Outstanding Balance Column** — Both vendor and customer list pages now show computed outstanding payable/receivable balance per entity
- **Color-coded balances** — Vendor payables highlighted in red, customer receivables in green

### Chart of Accounts
- **Grouped by Category** — Accounts are now organized into collapsible sections: Assets, Liabilities, Equity, Revenue, Expenses
- **Section Headers** — Each group shows an icon, label, and total balance for the category
- **Styled Account Codes** — Account codes displayed as monospace badges for scannability

### Collapsible Sidebar
- **Toggle Button** — New "← Collapse" / "→" toggle button in the sidebar footer
- **Icon Mode** — When collapsed, sidebar shows only emoji icons (72px wide) with tooltip support
- **Smooth Animation** — CSS transitions on width change for a polished feel
